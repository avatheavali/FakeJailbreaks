Methods of Jailbreaking

 

 

Untethered Jailbreak

Untethered is the permanent and real jailbreak solution. In this method, you will not lose the jailbreak when you reboot your iPhone.

 

Tethered Jailbreak

Tethered is a temporary jailbreak method. In this method, you have to proceed with the jailbreak process on your iPhone every time after rebooting.

 

Semi-Tethered Jailbreak

Semi-tethered is a combination of the above two jailbreak methods. In this method, you can restart your device with limited use, and you must run the jailbreak app to get the completely functional Cydia. It would help if you had a PC to run the jailbreak tool repeatedly in this method.

Semi-Untethered Jailbreak

This method is very much similar to the semi-tethered jailbreak method. The only difference between these two methods is that the semi-untethered jailbreak apps don’t ask a PC to run the jailbreak app.

Semi Jailbreak

Semi jailbreak apps allow you to install jailbreak tweaks, third-party apps, themes, and advanced apps with a system root access to your iPhone. They do not install Cydia. Instead, they install their in-built app stores. Intrix is such a semi jailbreak application that allows you to install exclusive apps and tweaks with system root access to your iPhone.
